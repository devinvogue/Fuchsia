Our Services

Eshyen Services

At ESHYEN we work with a number of professional drivers around the country in order to bring you a fully customizable BUS service. Wherever a driver and a car are needed, we can go. We focus particularly on transportation to and from ESHYEN main station, on business trips and on tourism, but are open to your individual requests too. Just send us an onlinev booking enquiry.

Custom tours

If you want to make the most of your stay in Eshyen by seeing as many sites as you can before you leave, think about booking a longer tour, courtesy of Eshyen. Our drivers can meet you on arrival in the country and accompany you for a few days - or for your whole trip. Our tours can be fully tailored to your needs.

However, our service is about more than just bringing you from one place to another. Our drivers and customer service agents are local people, who know the country better than anyone else. Not only can we advise you on the best itinerary for your trip, but you’ll benefit from local knowledge and expertise while on the road. If you’re interested in booking a custom tour with Eshyen, please fill out an enquiry form and tell us a little about your plans here.

Business transportation

If you are a company or organization looking to arrange travel for a group of people, or for a single employee, use our Eshyen to find the best transportation solution for your needs. If you’re organizing a trip for a larger group, we’re happy to help – we can send out two or more according to your needs.

Eshyen border transportation

Travelers visiting both Ghana and Togo or Cote D'ivore in one trip can travel from one country to the other via any of the three main official border crossing points.Eshyen can arrange to pick you up at the border and take you to your onward destination. Alternatively, if you are leaving Ghana, we will take you to the border.

Port's of Ghana services

Tourists arriving via cruise ship at the Port of Ghana can make the most of their short stay in Ghana by taking a day trip to Aburi Gardens or another tourist attraction by Eshyen. Our drivers will meet you at the port on your arrival, take you out on the road for the day, and bring you back again in time for the ship’s departure.
