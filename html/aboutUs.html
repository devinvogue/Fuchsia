About US

Eshyen Services

Eshyen is an online booking service that allows visitors to book a bus fare. Our company was started in 2018 with the intention of providing a quality service with affordable and transparent prices. We also offer day trips and longer tours around Ghana at highly competitive rates.

What you should know about ESHYEN:

  • Our service is tailored to tourists and travelers visiting Ghana and beyond
  • We work with several different professional drivers around the country, all of whom are local people with prior experience of driving tourists and citizens.
  • All drivers and customer service agents speak English , Fanti and Twi
  • Our customers can rely on us: we place great value on meeting you and getting you to your destination on time.
  • All vehicles are well maintained and undergo frequent safety checks
  • We have a perfect safety record
